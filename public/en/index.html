About the professional

Nice to meet you. I am Carla Suzana Marinho.

Clinical psychologist, neuropsychologist and cognitive behavioral therapist.

I hold a Psychology degree from UFGD, a Neuropsychology specialization from IBNeuro and training in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy from PUC-PR. For more than 12 years, I have supported teenagers, adults and families in mental health care.

My background also includes Human Development and Learning training at Unesp - Bauru and experience with children, teenagers, families and school contexts.

I work with Brazilians living in Brazil or abroad, as well as people from other nationalities who communicate in English. This has deepened my attention to adaptation, belonging, identity and new life paths.

Approach

Therapy as a safe space for listening, self-knowledge and meaningful change.

I see therapy as more than symptom relief. It can help each person live with more clarity, balance and purpose.

Frequently asked questions

Before scheduling

How does online therapy work?

Sessions take place by video call, in a private setting, with confidentiality and clear agreements about time, duration and frequency.

Do you work with teenagers?

Yes. Teen therapy considers development, family, school, emotions and life context.

Do you work with Brazilians living outside Brazil?

Yes. Online therapy allows support in Portuguese or English, with attention to cultural context and adaptation.

Can sessions be held in English?

Yes. Sessions can be held in Portuguese or English, including care for people from other nationalities who communicate in English.

How long are sessions?

Duration and frequency are discussed at the beginning of the therapeutic process.

Do you accept health insurance?

Please contact us on WhatsApp to confirm current payment and scheduling options.

How does payment work?

For patients in Brazil, payment can be made by Pix. For patients abroad, international payment instructions are sent privately after the appointment time is confirmed.

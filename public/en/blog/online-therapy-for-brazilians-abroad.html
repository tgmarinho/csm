Online therapy for Brazilians living abroad

Living in another country can bring growth and discovery, but it may also bring loneliness, cultural adaptation and distance from familiar references.

For many Brazilians abroad, being heard in Portuguese makes it easier to access emotions, memories and nuances of personal history. Language can be part of belonging.

Care across contexts

Online therapy can support people dealing with adaptation, identity, relationships, family distance, work pressure and transitions. Sessions happen by video call, with privacy and clear agreements.

Portuguese and English

Depending on the person's needs, sessions can happen in Portuguese or English, with attention to cultural context and emotional safety.

Anxiety - 4 min read

When does anxiety begin to ask for care?

Anxiety can be a natural reaction, but it may deserve attention when it becomes frequent, intense or limiting.

Worry is part of life. It can help us prepare, evaluate risks and organize decisions. The difficulty begins when the body remains on alert even in situations that do not require such intensity.

Signs to observe

Persistent tension, racing thoughts, irritability, sleep changes, avoidance and difficulty resting can indicate that anxiety is occupying too much space.

Therapy can help organize the experience

Psychological care does not promise to remove all anxiety. It can help understand triggers, identify thought patterns and build strategies that fit daily life.
